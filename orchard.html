The Orchard

You will not find a better income producing Orchard with such a diverse selection of fruits on three acres. There are 22 different fruits grown in the orchard. The "Taste the Fruits‚Äù section describes most of them.

The main commercial crops are Mango, Longan and Lychee.

Avocado, Dragon Fruit, Figs, and Malay Apple add a substantial amount to the harvest income too.

The other minor fruits, Abiu, Caimito, Custard Apple, Sapodilla, Carambola, Jackfruit, Durian, Langsat, Rambutan, Papaya, Passion Fruit, citrus and Jaboticaba add to the mix and will play a part in being able to provide a wide selection of tropical fruit to fruit platters the boutique resorts supply to their guest upon arrival.

Selections were chosen based on the Cook Islands weather conditions, proven commercial results in similar regions, and for their early, mid and late season harvesting times.

The nine mango varieties imported can extend the harvest season up to six months. These include R2E2, Kent, Kensington, Keitt, Irwin, King Thai, Nam Dok Mai, Palmer and Zillate.
