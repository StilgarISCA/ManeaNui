Return on Investment

Multiple income opportunities abound for this property, limited only by your imagination.

Established Income

Fruit Sales

Regardless of how you take ownership or structure your business, sales from the harvest will be achieved the first year of ownership.

Among the diversified agricultural products being produced today, exotic fruits provide for some of the highest net income per acre of land. The whole family will learn the business. From picking, packing, and delivering the fruit to maintaining the grounds and gardens, everyone will learn how to run the business and be proud of their achievements.

Don’t want to run the orchard? Management and staff to run the orchard can be hired and paid for from fruit sales and you will still have a healthy return on investment.

Orchard Tours

Orchard Tours are presently offered to tourist at $25 NZD per person, minimum four persons per tour. Tours could be a major source of income if promoted and advertised. Tours are currently limited based on requests.

Other Potential Income Sources

Long Term Rental Income

Not ready to leave your present home? Purchase now for the future and rent out Manea Nui Plantation to a long term renter.

Businesses on Rarotonga have a need for a high end rental homes for their executive employees, those on a multiple year contracts from New Zealand or Australia.

Bed and Breakfast

Taste paradise. Today’s traveller is more demanding than ever, wanting something new and exotic. The health conscious traveller is willing to pay a premium for the unusual, the exotic and the highest quality produce.

It only gets better. These are the dessert fruits, the fruits to die for, a taste of paradise!

The layout of this home offers an excellent opportunity to develop a bed and breakfast retreat with a tropical fruit theme. Bountiful fruit bowls on the kitchen counter overflowing with fragrant ready to eat exotic fruit, fresh from the orchards, available to your guest all day, what could be better.

Wedding Events

Weddings are a big business on Rarotonga and the floor plan of the home works perfectly for large groups; engagement parties, rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, and anniversary dinners.

Value Added Product

Besides being marketed and sold whole fresh, fruit can be processed for individual fruit salads, buffet display, flower arrangements, baked desserts, cooking ingredient, candy, puree, dried, syrup, and for jams and jellies just to name a few ideas.

Vacation Rental Income

Maybe you just want to have a vacation home where you can escape the New Zealand weather a week or two at a time. The flight from Auckland is short and in no time you could be relaxing on the deck soaking up the tropical heat.

Let a vacation rental company manage and maintain your property while you bank the income while still having a tropical escape of your very own. Schedule the time for yourself and earn income from the time not used. This home can demand a high rental income.

Orchard Brunch and Tour

Existing facilities provide for a unique “Luncheon and Tropical Fruit Orchard Tour”. At an estimated charge of $75 - $100 NZD per person, a group of four to ten individuals would make for a profitable mid days work. A healthy salad plate paired with fine wines and concluded with a sampling of today’s harvest of fresh fruit, you will be turning away guest due to advanced bookings. Too healthy!

Plant Material/Nursery Sales

For those of you with green thumbs, the demand for tropical fruit trees is increasing as the industry develops. Nursery income can be achieved from plant material sold to the emerging tropical fruit industry in the Cook Islands and to the backyard Gardner. At around $30 NZD per tree the income quickly grows to a healthy level.
